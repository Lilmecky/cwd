What it is

For Honor is a melee combat game where you fight using timing, reads, and matchups. It’s the kind of game where you feel like a genius one minute and then eat 3 heavies in a row the next.

Why I like it

The combat feels personal. When you win, it feels like you earned it. Also the style is just cool: knights, vikings, and samurais
